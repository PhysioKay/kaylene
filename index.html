MEET KAYLENE

Kaylene finds fulfillment in aiding her patients' fitness, lifestyle, and rehabilitation pursuits. Her approach integrates manual therapy, tailored exercise plans, patient education, and IMS techniques, aiming not only to treat but to empower.

Holding degrees in Biology and Physical Therapy from the University of British Columbia, Kaylene values continuous learning, expanding her expertise in various fields like the Mulligan Concept and Dry Needling. Beyond her profession, she enjoys outdoor activities like paddleboarding, biking, and hiking, fueled by her passion for travel, having explored destinations like Machu Picchu and Mount Fuji.

Kaylene eagerly anticipates the opportunity to support you in achieving your goals and fostering a healthy lifestyle.
